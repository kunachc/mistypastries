About Us

Misty Pastries, a budding gem in the world of confectionery, was founded by the remarkably talented 19-year-old, Ken Chico, hailing from the picturesque islands of the Philippines. At such a young age, Ken has managed to blend traditional Filipino flavors with contemporary baking techniques, resulting in a unique array of mouthwatering pastries. His passion for baking and commitment to authenticity has not only given rise to a brand that celebrates its roots but also introduces the world to the rich and diverse palate of the Philippines.
